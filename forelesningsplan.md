In particular, take note of the RStudio IDE Cheat Sheet and the Data Transformation Cheat Sheet.

Alternatively, you can access an online version of R through the Jupyter notebook interface [here](https://jupyter.uit.no){:target='_blank_'}. Use your UiT username and password.

If you're new to R or feel the need to polish your R skills, consider taking this free introductory course on [datacamp](https://www.datacamp.com/courses/free-introduction-to-r){:target='_blank_'}. A plethora of useful [CheatSheets](https://www.rstudio.com/resources/cheatsheets/){:target='_blank_'} are also available online.
If you google "learning r" you will find lots of sources. Introductory videos on "learning r" is also abundant on youtube.

published by Wiley in 2018. It is available at the campus bookstore, Akademika.
